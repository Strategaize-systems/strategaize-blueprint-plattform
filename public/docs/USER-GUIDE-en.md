# User Guide — Blueprint Assessment

Welcome to the user guide for the StrategAIze Blueprint Platform. This guide helps you use the platform effectively and complete the Exit Ready Blueprint.

## Welcome and Overview

### What is the Exit Ready Blueprint?

The Exit Ready Blueprint is a structured questionnaire with 67 questions across 9 thematic blocks. It analyses your company's exit readiness in areas such as business model, leadership, processes, finances and more. Your answers form the basis for strategic consulting by StrategAIze.

### How is the platform structured?

The platform consists of three main areas:

- **Dashboard** — Your personal overview with all assigned runs and their progress.
- **Workspace** — The working area where you answer questions, interact with the AI and upload documents.
- **Learning Center** — This help page with guides and video tutorials.

### What is a Run?

A run is an instance of the Blueprint questionnaire assigned to you by StrategAIze. Each run contains all 67 questions, organised in 9 blocks. You can work on a run across multiple sessions — your progress is saved automatically.

## Starting Your First Run

### Login and Dashboard

After receiving your invitation email, you can log in to the platform. First, set your password using the link in the email. After logging in, you will be taken directly to the dashboard.

### Opening a Run

On the dashboard, you will see your assigned runs as cards. Each card shows the title, current status, your progress and the number of uploaded evidence documents. Click on a run card to open the workspace and start working.

### Navigating the Workspace

In the workspace, you will see the block structure on the left with all 9 blocks (A to I). Click on a block to see the questions it contains. Click on a question to open it in the central area and start answering.

## Answering Questions

### Editing a Question

Select a question from the left navigation. In the central area, you will see the question text and an input field for your answer. Type your answer and click Save. Your answer is saved immediately.

### Multiple Answers per Question

You can submit multiple answers per question. Each new answer is added to the history — previous answers are preserved and numbered (#1, #2, ...). This is useful when you want to address different aspects of a question separately or add supplementary information later.

### Tracking Progress

In the left navigation, you can see progress per block. Answered questions are marked. The progress bar at the top shows your overall progress across all blocks.

### Submitting Blocks

When you have answered all questions in a block, you can submit it. Click the Submit button at the bottom of the workspace. After submission, your answers for that block are transmitted to StrategAIze.

## Using AI Support

### AI Follow-up Questions

The platform includes an integrated AI assistant. After you save an answer, the AI analyses your response and asks targeted follow-up questions when needed. These questions help you deepen your answer and add important details.

### Generating Summaries

You can ask the AI to create a summary of all your answers to a question. The summary consolidates all your individual answers into a structured overall text. This is particularly useful when you have submitted multiple answers across different sessions.

### Using Voice Input

Instead of typing, you can also speak your answers. Click the microphone icon next to the input field. Recording starts and your speech is automatically converted to text. You can edit the text before sending. Speech recognition supports German, English and Dutch.

### Tips for Effective AI Usage

- **The more detailed your answer, the better the follow-ups** — The AI can only work based on your input. Provide as many relevant details as possible.
- **Use the follow-up questions** — The AI follow-ups are designed to identify gaps in your answers. Answer them to improve quality.
- **Speak naturally** — When using voice input, you can speak freely. The AI transcribes your words and you can refine the text afterwards.

## Uploading Documents

### Uploading Evidence

You can upload relevant documents as evidence for any question. Supported formats are PDF, DOCX and TXT. Click the upload area on the right side of the workspace, select a file and assign a label (e.g. "Contract", "Financial Report", "Process Documentation").

### Labels and Categories

Each uploaded document receives a label describing what type of evidence it is. Available labels include: Policy, Process, Template, Contract, Financial Document, Legal Document, System Documentation, Organisation Chart, KPI Report and Other.

### Document Analysis by AI

After uploading, the AI automatically analyses your document in the context of the current question. You receive feedback on what information the document contributes to answering the question and whether aspects remain open. This analysis appears in the question history.

## Checking Progress and Submitting Blocks

### Reviewing Your Progress

In the workspace, the sidebar always shows your progress per block. Answered questions are visually marked. The progress indicator gives you a quick overview of which areas are still open.

### Submitting a Block

When you are done with a block, you can submit it for review. The Submit button appears at the bottom of the workspace. After submission, you see a confirmation with the number of answered questions. You can optionally add a note to the submission.

### After Submission

Submitted blocks are reviewed by StrategAIze. The status changes from "In Progress" to "Submitted". As long as the block is not locked, you can continue to add answers and resubmit.

## Tips for Better Results

### Quality over Quantity

It is better to give few but detailed answers than many superficial ones. The AI follow-up questions help you reach the right depth.

### Use Documents

Upload relevant documents wherever possible. The AI can analyse these and identify connections that complement your text answers.

### Work at Your Own Pace

You do not need to complete the Blueprint in a single session. Work block by block and use the automatic saving. Your progress is never lost.

### Data Privacy

All your data is stored securely on European servers. AI processing also takes place locally on our servers — your data never leaves the EU. Audio recordings are only used for transcription and deleted immediately afterwards.
